# Full Rebrand & Digital Transformation. Strategy to Execution.

A growing B2B technology company came to us with a familiar problem. Their brand had evolved piecemeal over the years. Three different logo variations were in active use. Color palettes conflicted across their website, sales decks, and social media. Their WordPress site was slow and outdated. We led a 10-week engagement to overhaul everything. The team designed a new logo and a complete visual identity system with full brand guidelines covering typography, color, voice, and application rules. We rebuilt the website from scratch on Next.js and Tailwind CSS, delivering a fast, responsive experience. A unified content and social media strategy brought consistency across every channel. Brand consistency improved across all touchpoints. Website traffic tripled. Customer surveys showed measurable gains in brand perception. The entire transition happened without disrupting their day-to-day operations.
