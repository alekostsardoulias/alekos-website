# Marketing Automation Pipeline for SaaS

A B2B SaaS company needed to scale their lead nurturing without scaling their marketing team. We built a fully automated pipeline that handles everything from lead capture to sales handoff.

## The Solution

The pipeline integrates HubSpot CRM with custom email sequences built on Resend. Lead scoring is powered by a combination of behavioral triggers (website visits, content downloads, email engagement) and firmographic data. When a lead hits a threshold score, they're automatically routed to the sales team with a pre-filled context card.

## Impact

- 300+ leads automatically nurtured per month with zero manual intervention
- Email open rates increased from 22% to 34%
- Sales team reported 25% faster deal cycles due to better-qualified leads
- Fully documented and handed off to the in-house team for ongoing management
