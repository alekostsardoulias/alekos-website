# AI-Powered Analytics Dashboard. From Spreadsheets to Intelligence.

A mid-sized B2B company was running their reporting on separate Excel sheets and Google Docs across marketing, sales, and finance. Each week teams spent over 40 hours manually consolidating data from their CRM, ad platforms, and accounting software. We built a custom internal dashboard that ingests all data sources automatically. Machine learning models detect spend anomalies and forecast revenue by channel in real time. Managers ask questions in plain English, like "show me Q3 revenue by region," and get instant visual answers. The project went from kickoff to deployment in eight weeks. Reporting time dropped by 90%. In its first week, the anomaly detection flagged a 30% budget overspend that would have gone unnoticed until month-end.
