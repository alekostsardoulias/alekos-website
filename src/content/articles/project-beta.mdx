# E-Commerce Replatform. Legacy to Modern Stack

The client was running on an aging platform that was slow, hard to maintain, and limiting growth. We replatformed the entire store to a modern JAMstack architecture, carefully migrating product data, customer records, and order history. The new platform loads in under two seconds and has seen a 40% improvement in conversion rates since launch.
